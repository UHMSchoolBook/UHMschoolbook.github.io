Motivation

Whenever a new student starts their journey in a university, their first challenge is to create connectivity with other students. Even older students face this issue in the form of new courses and clubs. The motivation behind this app is to decrease the gap between students on campus while allowing various socializing opportunities designed specifically for the students of UHM.

Goals

The goal of the “Meet Your People!!” is to improve connectivity in student communities by providing a platform that is specifically designed for students, especially for a particular campus. This app has options to interact as individuals, groups, and members of classes with each other. There are Marketplace, House sharing, and messaging modules to alleviate the college experience of every student.

Usage

  • Login Page

    This page shows up at the start of the app and allows users to login into the app with their credentials.

  • registration page

    This page allows new users to create an account in the app so that they can get connected to their peers in the same courses and groups.

  • Feed page for Clubs

    This page shows feeds posted by clubs including updates by moderator and other students.

  • Marketplace page for Items

    The marketplace page displays listing of items put to sale by different students

  • Housesharing

    Housesharing allows students who want to share their rooms and house with other students for rent sharing the option to do so.

  • Live Activity

    Students can share what they are doing at the moment (eg. songs, movie, book) with other students through this section.

  • Messages

    This page allows students to send messages to each other in a secure way
