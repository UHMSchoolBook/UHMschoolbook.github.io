Motivation

Whenever a new student start their journey in a university their first challange is to create connectivity with other students. Even older students face this issue in the form of new courses and clubs. The motivation behind this app is to decrease the gap between students in campus while allowing various socializing opprtunities designed specifically for the students of UHM.

Goals

The goal of the “Meet Your People!!” is to improve the connectivity in student communities by providing a platform which is specifically designed for students especially for a particular campus. This app has options to interact as individuals, groups and member of classes with each other. There are Marketplace, House sharing and messaging modules to alleviate the college experience of every student.
